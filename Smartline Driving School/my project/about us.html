Our journey to Excellence

founded in 2015, Smartline driving school began as a small training center for drivers with a mission of creating confident and responsible drivers in Uganda. For the 10 years we have been in existence, we have trained over 5000 drivers who are now successful and have employed due to their driving skills forexample some have been employed in sectoRs like Tourism, logistics and so many others. Today we continue with our mordern training methods, including simulator training and defensive driving techniques,while maintaining our commttiment to affordable, quality education for all.
